Larry Luetke for Minnehaha County Commissioner

I've spent 20 years helping families put down roots in Minnehaha County. Now I want to protect what makes it a great place to live — reasonable taxes, disciplined growth, and safe communities.

My Priorities

💰

Property Taxes

Homeowners deserve relief from rising property tax burdens. I'll fight for responsible budgeting that respects your hard-earned money while maintaining essential services.

🏗️

Disciplined Growth

Minnehaha County is growing fast — and growth without discipline costs taxpayers. I'll fight for infrastructure that keeps pace with development, responsible land use, and long-term planning that doesn't saddle future generations with debt.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Safe Communities

Every family deserves to feel safe in their neighborhood. I'll support our law enforcement, back proven public safety programs, and ensure Minnehaha County has the resources needed to protect our residents.

Why I'm Running

Since 2003, I've worked with hundreds of families in Minnehaha County — helping them find homes, put down roots, and build their lives here. I've seen firsthand what makes this community special.

But I've also seen the challenges: rising costs, growing pains from rapid development, and concerns about the future. I believe we need practical, common-sense leadership that puts families first.

I'm not a career politician. I'm a lifelong South Dakotan who cares deeply about this place. I want to bring my experience working with people and solving problems to the County Commission.

I'd be honored to earn your vote.
